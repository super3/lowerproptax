Frequently asked questions

Once you receive your report, you submit it along with your appeal form to your county assessor's office. The report provides the comparable property evidence needed to support your case for a lower assessment.

Appeal deadlines vary by county in Georgia, typically 45 days after you receive your assessment notice. We recommend starting your assessment with us as soon as you receive your notice.

If our analysis shows your property is fairly assessed or we can't find comparable properties that support a lower value, you pay nothing. The initial assessment is always free.

We currently serve homeowners throughout Georgia. We're working on expanding to other states soon. Enter your address to see if we can help with your property.

Most reports are ready within 24 hours of entering your property. We'll email you as soon as your assessment is complete and ready to download.
